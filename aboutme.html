About me

Hello there, my name is Damir Chaban! I am 14 year old ukranian. I am a junior Web developer, editor, and a singer.

I create Websites, graphic designs, music, videos overall more than 5 years. I always do my best to improve myself and give people the most accurate and high-quality product.

Web-Development career

It all started in autumn of 2024, when i found out that in the IT school, where i was learning MC-Java previosly, a Frontend group just started. I was interested in creating websites, so i agreed to join the group. Throughout the course, i learned HTML, CSS, and Scss. The course still goes on, and i am constantly improving my skills. I have already created a few websites, including this portfolio, and i am ready to create more websites for people who need them. There were a few breaking points where i wanted to quit, because sometimes the course seemed to be extra hard, but i still go on.
